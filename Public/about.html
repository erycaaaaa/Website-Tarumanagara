Tarumanagara
Foundation

Established in 1959, Tarumanagara Foundation has a rich history of providing quality education and healthcare to Indonesians through Tarumanagara University (Untar), Tarumanagara Xinya College (Xinya), Institut Tarumanagara (Itaru), Royal Taruma Hospital, PT Taruma Bhakti Utama, and other corporations.

Our university emphasizes 3 values

to enhance students learning experience towards a better future for Indonesia and the world.

Integrity

Professionalism

Entrepreneurship

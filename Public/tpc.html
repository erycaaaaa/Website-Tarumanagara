Tarumanagara
Professional Center

The Tarumanagara Professional Center (TPC) at Universitas Tarumanagara (UNTAR) is a professional development institution established in 2000 by Yayasan Tarumanagara. TPC is dedicated to enhancing students' skills and competencies, helping them meet industry standards and preparing them for successful careers.

Our Services

TPC offers various courses aimed at improving both technical and soft skills. Students can participate in workshops, seminars, and hands-on training sessions designed to boost their leadership, communication, and problem-solving abilities.
